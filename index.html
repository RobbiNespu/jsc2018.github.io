About the Conference

This conference is the first of its kind in Malaysia, where several specialists societies have joint hands to conduct it.. These societies are: College of Dental Specialists (CODS), Academy of Medicine Malaysia; Malaysian Association of Oral and Maxillo-facial Surgery (MAOMS); Malaysian Association of Dental Public Health Specialists (MADPHS); Malaysian Association of Paediatric Dentistry (MAPD); Malaysian Association for Oro-Facial Diseases (MAOFD), Malaysian Society of Periodontology (MSP) and Malaysian Association of Prosthodontists (MAP).

Theme/Topics

The theme: “DENTAL SPECIALTIES: TOWARDS A SUCCESSFUL TOMORROW”, is general enough to encompasses all specialties and how in many situations combine management can be beneficial to the patients. The organizer has assembled excellent speakers from all over the world to share their knowledge and experience. This conference will also be a good platform for the different specialties to present, share and discuss each aspect of the topics in focus.

The topics in focus of this conference are :

  • PAIN
  • HYPODONTIA
  • DENTAL PUBLIC HEALTH and RESEARCH
  • etc.

So, why wait. Book your dates and come and join this awesome conference.
